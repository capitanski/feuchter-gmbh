Purpose

The two-piece operational suit consists of an operational jacket and operational trousers and is intended for officers of the Federal Police. It is worn over the Federal Police's body protection equipment.

Brief Description – Operational Jacket

  • With stand-up collar, concealed front zipper, four pockets
  • Four access openings and two sleeve closures for securing body protection equipment
  • With elastic drawcord at the hem for width adjustment
  • With sleeve patches and police identification
  • Operational trousers with detachable suspenders and adjustable hem construction
  • With side, back, thigh, and lower leg pockets
  • With inner leg zippers for securing body protection equipment

Operational Jacket and Trousers

The design, wearing comfort, and fit of the jacket and trousers must be matched and functionally coordinated with each other.

High-Quality Aramid Fabric

Aramid fabric is particularly known for the following properties:

  1. High tensile strength – Aramid fibers are extremely resistant to tensile forces and offer high durability.
  2. Lightweight – Despite its strength, aramid is very light.
  3. Heat resistance – It withstands high temperatures and only begins to degrade at around 500 °C.
  4. Flame-retardant – It doesn’t burn but chars when exposed to high heat.
  5. Chemical resistance – Resistant to many organic solvents but sensitive to strong acids and bases.
  6. High impact resistance – Withstands shocks and punctures, making it ideal for protective clothing and ballistic applications.
  7. Low electrical conductivity – Used in electrical and thermal insulation applications.

Flame-Retardant Properties

  • Protection against heat & flames: The material is flame-retardant and offers protection when exposed to fire or intense heat.
  • Integrated flame protection: The flame-retardant property is embedded in the fiber itself (not a chemical coating that washes out).
  • Meets safety standards: Developed for operations with increased risk potential, e.g., demonstrations or large-scale events.

Membrane-Free – Breathable & Comfortable

  • High breathability: As the suit does not contain a membrane, it allows excellent air circulation. Ideal for physically demanding operations.
  • Quick-drying & moisture-wicking: Sweat is effectively transported outward, creating a comfortable wearing climate.
  • Lightweight: Without a membrane, the fabric remains lighter and more flexible than waterproof versions.

High Durability & Functionality

  • Durable material: Developed for daily, demanding use in police service.
  • Ergonomic cut: Optimal freedom of movement for dynamic operations.
  • Tactical pocket layout: Multiple functional pockets for equipment like gloves, notepad, flashlight, or radio.
  • Reinforced seams & abrasion protection: Especially at heavily stressed areas like knees and elbows.

Color Scheme & Official Standards

  • Color: Dark blue – matching the uniform of general police forces.
  • Tested according to the Technical Delivery Conditions (TL) of the Federal Ministry of the Interior: Meets requirements for police operational clothing in Germany.

Compatibility with Protective Gear

  • Compatible with body armor & tactical equipment.
  • Suitable for a wide range of scenarios: Everyday duty, major events, demonstrations, traffic checks, etc.
