Brief Description

Quilted jacket with a divisible zipper with cover in the collar area, set-in sleeves, knitted collar and sleeve cuffs, attached right zipper chest pocket, underarm ventilation, drawstring at the hem.

‍

Thermal Insulation & Material

  • High-Quality Insulation: Made from modern, thermally insulating materials like Climashield® or similar synthetic fibers that reliably provide warmth even in extremely cold temperatures.
  • Breathable & Moisture-Wicking: The material wicks moisture away from the body, keeping the wearer dry and warm.
  • Windproof: The outer layer protects against cold wind and enhances thermal performance.

‍

Lightweight & Compact

  • Low Weight: Despite high thermal performance, the jacket remains lightweight and non-bulky.
  • Compactly Stowable: Can be packed down small, ideal for modular use with other Bundeswehr clothing items.

‍

Ergonomic Design & Comfort

  • Underlayer Function: Designed as a mid-layer for the layering principle, but can also be worn independently.
  • High Freedom of Movement: Special cut for military requirements to ensure good mobility.
  • High Collar & Elastic Sleeve Ends: Additional protection against cold and wind.

‍

Robust & Military-Grade Construction

  • Tested According to TL 8415-0234: Meets the stringent technical delivery conditions (TL) of the Bundeswehr for cold protection clothing.
  • Durable Workmanship: Resistant to wear and stress in use.
  • Compatible with Other Bundeswehr Equipment: Can be combined with other layers (e.g., cold protection overall, rain protection jacket).

‍

Camouflage & Color Adaptation

  • Color: Typically held in olive or stone gray-olive to support the camouflage effect in use.

‍

This jacket is therefore specifically optimized for the requirements of the Bundeswehr – functional, durable, and ideal for cold operations.

Underlayer Cold Protection Pants According to TL 8415-0260

The Underlayer Cold Protection Pants according to TL 8415-0260 are special cold protection equipment of the Bundeswehr with the following special features:

‍

Excellent Thermal Insulation

  • Effective Insulation: Made from modern synthetic insulation materials like Climashield® or similar high-performance fibers that provide optimal warmth even at extreme temperatures.
  • Heat Retention with Low Weight: Keeps the body warm without being bulky or restricting mobility.

‍

Breathability & Moisture Management

  • Moisture-Wicking: Wicks sweat to the outside, keeping the wearer dry.
  • Breathable: Prevents overheating and ensures a comfortable wearing climate.

‍

Ergonomic & Functional Design

  • Underlayer Function: Designed as a mid-layer for the layering principle, can be worn under regular clothing.
  • High Freedom of Movement: Special cut tailored to military requirements.
  • Elastic Waistband & Leg Ends: Ensures a good fit and minimizes heat loss.

‍

Robust & Bundeswehr-Compatible

  • Meets TL 8415-0260: Developed according to the technical delivery conditions (TL) of the Bundeswehr for cold protection clothing.
  • Durable & Resistant: Resistant to abrasion, stress, and long deployments.
  • Compatible with Other Cold Protection Equipment: Can be combined with the underlayer cold protection jacket and other Bundeswehr clothing items.

‍

Camouflage & Color Adaptation

  • Color: Held in olive or stone gray-olive to support the camouflage effect.

‍

Conclusion

The underlayer cold protection pants according to TL 8415-0260 are lightweight, highly insulating, and functional pants that are optimally suited for military use in cold conditions. They combine thermal performance, comfort, and durability – perfect for demanding conditions.
