The blouson is made with a stand-up collar, concealed front zipper, 2 chest and 2 side pockets, 5 access openings for securing body armor, a tab for attaching a radio antenna, sleeve seam zippers, elastic sleeve cuffs, and a drawcord at the hem for adjustment. Below the left chest pocket, a Velcro area is sewn on for attaching a name badge. Both the front and back sections feature the “POLIZEI” lettering attached with Velcro, and the sleeves have Velcro patches for attaching rank and national emblems. On the back, Velcro bands are sewn on in a square formation to allow tactical identifiers to be attached. The trousers feature a fixed waistband, belt loops, detachable suspenders, 2 side pockets, 2 back pockets, and 2 thigh and 2 lower-leg pockets, along with an adjustable hem. Inside, the front trousers include two Velcro-sealed insertion pockets for thigh protection. For securing shin protection, the legs are equipped with inner seam zippers. The front knee areas are additionally reinforced from the outside.
